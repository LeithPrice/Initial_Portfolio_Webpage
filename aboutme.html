About me

Work / Study History

Since completing year 12 in 1999, I have been self employed for the better part of 20 years. Specializing in local and interstate transport services. Being the head of a large employer, i have had extensive experience in managing different personalities and encouraging them to give their best. As i began to wind up my interests in Transport i did RPL and got my qualifications in Heavy Vehicle Mechanics, Auto Electrics and Automotive airconditioning. For the past three years i have enjoyed a healthy balance of work time and family time for the first time in my career.

I am currently undertaking Training to complete my Diploma in IT with the intention of entering a rewarding career that will compliment my experiences at this stage in my life.
